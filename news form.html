May 15, 2023

UEP Marks Golden Jubilee with Week-long Celebration

The University of Eastern Philippines commemorated its 50th founding anniversary with a series of events including an alumni homecoming, academic symposiums, and cultural showcases.

Read more

The University of Eastern Philippines commemorated its 50th founding anniversary with a series of events including an alumni homecoming, academic symposiums, and cultural showcases. The week-long celebration culminated in a grand gala attended by distinguished alumni and government officials.

The celebration kicked off with a torch parade, symbolizing the light of knowledge that UEP has been spreading for half a century. Throughout the week, various departments organized exhibitions showcasing student projects and faculty research. A highlight of the celebration was the 'UEP Through the Decades' photo exhibit, which drew many nostalgic visitors.

The gala dinner saw emotional reunions and inspiring speeches from successful alumni, emphasizing UEP's role in shaping leaders across various fields. The university also took this opportunity to launch its 'Vision 2073' plan, outlining ambitious goals for the next 50 years.

June 2, 2023

UEP Unveils State-of-the-Art Science Laboratory Complex

UEP proudly inaugurated its new Bio-Physical Sciences Laboratory Complex, featuring cutting-edge equipment and research facilities.

Read more

UEP proudly inaugurated its new Bio-Physical Sciences Laboratory Complex, featuring cutting-edge equipment and research facilities. This P50-million project aims to boost the university's research capabilities and support advanced scientific studies.

The complex houses separate wings for Biology, Chemistry, and Physics, each equipped with the latest technology in their respective fields. The Biology wing features a genomics lab and a greenhouse for plant studies. The Chemistry wing boasts a state-of-the-art spectroscopy lab, while the Physics wing includes an advanced optics laboratory.

This new facility is expected to significantly enhance UEP's capacity for groundbreaking research and attract top-tier faculty and students. The university has also announced plans for collaborative research projects with international institutions, leveraging this new infrastructure.
